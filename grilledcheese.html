Grilled cheese sandwich

Prep time: 10 min

Yield: 4

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Stir together butter and permasan in a small bowl.
  2. Spread 1.1/2 tsp butter mixture on 1 side of each bread slice. place 4 bread slices,buttered sides down on wax paper top with provolone and mozarell cheese top with remaining bread slices buyttered side up.
  3. Cook sandwiches in batches on a grilled or in a non stick skillet over midium het gently pressing with a spatulla 4 on each side oruntil golden brown and cheese is smelted.
